9. Details About the Services

Psychological tests

The Anima Felix Mobile Application offers Users the possibility to access a variety of psychological tests specially designed to assess different aspects of mental and emotional health. After completing the tests, Users receive detailed analysis and personalized recommendations that can help them better understand their psychological state and identify directions for improvement. These tools are designed to support self-awareness and personal development and are accessible in an easy and user-friendly way directly from the Mobile Application.

Journal

The diary functionality provides a private and secure space where users can record their daily mood, observations about their psycho-emotional state, a personal note and a representative photo. Based on the information entered, the Mobile Application generates a personalized analysis, providing insights into emotional fluctuations and overall well-being over time. In addition, users can view their log history and statistics on how their mood has evolved over time.

Users are prohibited from uploading photos that include their face, other people's faces, or images that reference illegal, sexual, violent or self-mutilating activities. The Anima Felix Mobile Application utilizes an automated filter that prevents the uploading of photos containing illegal images, ensuring compliance with the rules and maintaining a safe environment for Users.
